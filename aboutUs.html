  • our story

    Born in 1997, Harry Barker has been combining our love of pets with our appreciation of fashion and style for decades.We focus on classic design elements that compliment your home décor as much as they please your pet. Our timeless designs convey our passion for clean-living and our dedication for a better planet yet are all infused with a playful sense of whimsy. Using recycled and sustainable materials, while supporting organizations with our give-back initiatives—Harry Barker’s products strengthen the connection between people, their pets, and their planet.

  • the harry barker difference

    We share a commitment to improve the lives of pets, people and our planet.

  • our promise

    Sure, our products are fun and functional, but many of them are made of sustainable materials that have been re-purposed and given a second chance. In turn, their products will often end up in the homes of pets who have also been given a second chance. It’s a life cycle that keeps on giving back. We use earth- friendly fabrics and azo-free dyes, eco-fleece and “green” bed inserts made from 100% post-consumer recycled plastics, as well as all-natural ingredients for treats and biodegradable shampoos.

  • why our products?

    eco-friendly

    Many of our products are made from recycled materials using sustainable manufacturing processes

    beautiful

    Pleasing to you and your pet, our products are meant to compliment your home's décor.

    well made

    Nothing is more important to us than the quality and integrity of our products.

  • giving back

    Harry Barker has a long history of giving back by donating a portion of proceeds from the sale of select products to other like-minded organizations and causes who also stand behind pets, people and the world we live in. Current partners include the Canines for Veterans, Mote Marine Laboratory and Planet Bee Foundation. Visit our give back page for more information.
