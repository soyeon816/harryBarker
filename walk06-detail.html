Based on our long-term heritage of creating beautiful, high-quality and sustainable products, the new Harry Barker Gingham Collection combines fresh- from-nature neutrals, a timeless tan and cream gingham pattern and planet- friendly materials. The line offers a fresh take on our signature Harry Barker collars, leashes and accessories.

Your pup’s collar is their everyday wear. As such it needs to be EVERYTHING… Our collars are high-quality, lightweight, super durable, machine washable and dry almost instantly - perfect for an outing. As functional as they are beautiful, the YKK quick-release buckles are ergonomic, contoured to fit your pet and can withstand up to 600 pounds of pressure.
