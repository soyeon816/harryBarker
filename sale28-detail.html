Welcome country charm into your home with the Grain Sack Dog Bed from Harry Barker. One of three stripe colors—black, red, or blue—is set against a rustic canvas that looks great in any home. This timeless print gives a nod to America’s family farmers and provides the perfect place for country and city dogs alike to take a nap.

This rectangle dog bedwelcomes your pup to find a soft spot to lay down with plump, durable bed inserts that are made in the USA. Our high-quality inserts are channel quilted and generously stuffed, so they won’t lose their shape over time.
